Clean Water

Water is a cross-cutting issue for future development of our Nation,with groundwater blocks are to be in the semi-critical, critical or overexploited categories, with the situation rapidly deteriorating. At the same time, diversion and contamination of surface water threatens the health of rivers and the people dependent upon them. Emergent Materials such as several forms of Carbon can be modified to increase their surface area and chemistry to improve on their sorption rate,such as to not only remove contamination such as heavy metal but reduce the energy costs and dependency. We strive to make the environment more sustainable by use of modern nano-materials changing the way you drink water.

Filtration

A rapidly growing economy needs energy to meet its growth objectives in a sustainable manner. The increasing energy requirements coupled with a slower than expected increase in domestic fuel production has rendered India among the top five Green-house-gas (GHG) emitters globally. Lesser efficient energy storage systems,high thermal run offs and toxic materials adds on as technological barrier in the road to sustainable development. Newer storage technologies coupled with transformation of science into technology will lead the present into a future with clean energy,and groundBlu pledges to be the torch bearer carrying the mantle of diversified implementation of present renewable energy sources coupled with better systems management by the application of nano science.
